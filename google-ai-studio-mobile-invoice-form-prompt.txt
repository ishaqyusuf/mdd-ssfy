SUMMARY CALCULATION

Implement local calculateSummary compatible with backend meaning:

- subTotal: sum of lineItems.lineTotal
- discount: sum extraCosts where type is "Discount"
- discountPct: sum extraCosts where type is "DiscountPercentage"
- percentDiscountValue: subTotal * discountPct / 100
- labor: sum "Labor" and "FlatLabor" costs
- delivery: sum "Delivery"
- otherCosts: all other custom/EXT costs
- taxableSubTotal: taxable line totals + taxable delivery/custom costs - discounts, never below 0
- taxTotal: taxableSubTotal * taxRate / 100
- adjustedSubTotal: subTotal - discount - percentDiscountValue + labor + delivery + otherCosts
- grandTotal: adjustedSubTotal + taxTotal
- Round currency to 2 decimals.

Remember line item field is taxxable.
