## Worker Usability Review

- Sales reps: Sales Overview Inventory has better language around locked/non-applicable states, but the action area is still not trainable enough because Create inbound and Mark all available do not expose complete eligibility guidance.
- Production staff: Inventory readiness remains the right direction, but Brain still says clean reconciliation and operator proof are incomplete. Production should not treat the new inventory status as sole truth until Phase 8 and browser/operator proof close.
