# Define content, service, policy, SEO, and legacy migration

**Type:** research

**Status:** implemented; production content approval pending

**Blocked by:** Define embedded admin controls for storefront offers and
merchandising; Define customer offer pages and simplified configuration
journeys

## Question

Which legacy pages, products, categories, media, contact/custom-quote flows,
policies, newsletter behavior, URLs, metadata, structured data, and redirects
must be curated and migrated for a safe `gndmillwork.com` replacement?

## Comments

Build a curated migration inventory for the existing site covering pages,
product/category URLs, media, metadata, contact information, service content,
account routes, shipping information, terms, privacy, and returns.

The migration should:

- map legacy URLs to Storefront Offers, categories, content pages, or permanent
  redirects;
- migrate usable product/media content without importing $0 prices, stale
  promotions, placeholders, or unsupported claims;
- add functional contact and custom-quote intake with durable records, uploads,
  spam protection, assignment, and confirmation;
- publish approved shipping, pickup, warranty, return/refund, privacy/cookie,
  terms, and accessibility policies;
- implement sitemap, robots, canonical metadata, product/breadcrumb structured
  data, Open Graph data, and 404 monitoring;
- preserve high-value SEO paths through tested redirects;
- provide newsletter consent and unsubscribe only when an approved provider and
  policy exist.

Content publication may use structured merchandising controls, but it must not
become a second source for product compatibility or pricing.
